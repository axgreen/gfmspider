Chemo & Vet Services for Rogue

$1,205 of $1,000 goal

Raised by 26 people in 33 months
Donate Now  Share on Facebook
 Share
  Tweet
 23 shares
  • Story
  • Updates   1
Jennifer Lynne
on behalf of Celena Zevnik
 CLEVELAND, OH
This is Rogue, my best friend, Celena's, dog. Rogue was just diagnosed with stomach cancer after a series of tests this past week. With chemotherapy and possibly a surgery, we can save Rogue's life. As one of Celena's best friends, I'm simply asking for my friends to donate $10. That's it.
It was only 2 months ago as my apartment building was in flames, that my best friend was frantic trying to save my dog, Bentley, from a burning building as I was at work oblivious. If you've ever gotten tickets through me for anything at the Q, it was through Celena. This is our time to help her and Rogue through his tough time. If you have a doggie, please look at him/her and think of Rogue and please help him beat his cancer. Xoxo
