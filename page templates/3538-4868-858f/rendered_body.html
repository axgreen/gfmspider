Bekim Lumi - we need you back!

28,370 of 100,000 goal

Created February 28, 2018
Rebeka Aneq
on behalf of Luljeta Lumi
  Medical   Krailling, Bayern, Deutschland
Bekim Lumi, our talented dear friend, professor at the Faculty of Arts in Prishtina, a theatre director is battling cancer for the past two years.

The expensive treatment is  proving to be unbearable for his family!

He is currently awaiting for his next round of treatment which depends from us, all of us!

Bekim  needs our support, now that he faces a critical moment - moreover WE need him among us!

Please donate, any amount you can! We also need your moral support and prayers!

Luljeta Lumi - Bekim's sister that lives in Germany where Bekim will continue with treatment is the only beneficiary with access to collected funds!


Beko - come back! Family, friends, your actors and your audience is waiting!

Thank you all immensely!


Q prej dy vjetsh regjisori i mirnjohur dhe Profesori i Fakultetit t Arteve t UP-s, Bekim Lumi, sht duke u prball me smundjen e rnd t kancerit.

Gjat ksaj kohe, shpenzimet e trajtimit t gjat mjeksor n Gjermani kan qen jashtzakonisht t mdha dhe ato, me sforcim t madh, jan prballuar vetm nga familja e Bekimit.

Tash ka ardh nj moment kritik, kur, pr shkak t shpenzimeve t mdha financiare, atij i duhet mbshtjetja jon m e madhe pr ta vazhduar prballjen me smundjen e kancerit. 

Prandaj krkojm prkrahjen tuaj! Ndihmoni me aq sa mundeni, sado e madhe apo e vogl t jet shuma!
Mos harroni, Bekimit i duhet edhe prkrahja morale dhe te mbani n lutjet e juaja!

Luljeta Lumi, motra e Bekimit e cila jeton n Gjermani, ku trajtimi ka pr t vazhduar, sht personi i vetm q ka qasje n fondet me t cilat po kontribuoni aq zemrgjersisht.

Ju falemnderit t gjithve, pafundsisht!

Update 6
Posted by Rebeka Qena
1 month ago
    Share
 
 
Bekimi sht i fort! E kemi ditur dhe e dijm kt! Ai me kurajo po prballet me smundjen e rnd t kancerit dhe vazhdon t qndroj i fort. Ka vazhduar me administrim t terapis t ciln e kan mundsuar kontributet e juaja.
Bekimi vazhdon t ket nevoj pr ndihmn ton dhe ne ju lusim t vazhdoni t ndihmoni me aq sa mundeni! Sidoqoft, me gzim po ndajm nj lajm t mir me t gjith ju. Po ju njoftojm se cka po mundson ndihma juaj!
Bekimi ka koh q po punon n inskenimin e drams Arturo Ui t shkruar nga Bertolt Brecht, ngadal, e me shum ndrprerje pr shkak t gjendjes shndetsore.
Prkundr t gjitha vshtirsive, jemi t lumtur t ju njoftojm se prkushtimi i tij n pun, vullneti i tij dhe prkrahja e kolegve t tij po shpaguhet dhe se me datn 14 qershor kjo shfaqje do t ket premiern n Teatrin Kombtar t Kosovs.
Ju po e bni t mundur kt! Ju faleminderit t gjithve! Bjm apel t vazhdoni me ndihmn tuaj, kemi nevoj pr te! Bekimi ka nevoj pr te!

Bekim is strong! We knew this and we know it! He is fiercely battling the illness and is standing strong. He has continued receiving therapy, which was made possible by your contributions.
Bekim continues to need our support, therefore please keep donating, however big or small the donation is! Every bit helps!
However, here is an update as to what you have helped him achieve!
Bekim has been working in staging Arturo Ui by Bertolt Brecht, slowly, with many delays due to his health condition. However, we are happy to announce that the hard work, his courageousness, his will and the support of his co-workers are paying off and the audience will be able to enjoy this play from June the 14th, at the National Theatre of Kosovo, which is when the opening night is set for.
You are making this happen! Thank you ALL! Please keep up with the support! We need it! Bekim needs our support!
