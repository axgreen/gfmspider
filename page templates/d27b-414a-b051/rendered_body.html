Jade's medical fund.

£210 of £250 goal

Raised by 10 people in 32 months
Donate Now  Share on Facebook
 Share

  • Updates   3
JT
Jade Thorpe  Brentford, Greater London, United Kingdom
My name is Jade and I am 29.  Some people may think I have a cheek for asking others to help but I am at a point in my life were I have no choice!

I can no longer live my life this way! By asking for your help I hope to get answers and some kind of quality of life back for me and my 3 children!

I have up to now had 6 operations on my back due to a condtion called Cludia Equnia Syndrome!  Never heard of it?  No?  Neither had I till last year, until it took over my whole life and body and left me in constant pain, some days I can't even get up in the mornings!

I am 29 and I can no longer use the toilet, I have to use a bag , have lost muscle tone in my bottom so that will soon be next to go and may leave me with a bag!  I am on so many pain medicatons that I am sick most of the time.  Doctor after doctor they just keep feeding me with high dose drugs!

I have 3 Children to look after Angel 12 who is a huge help but shouldn't be cleaning up my wet sheets or call 999 on a weekly basis!  Rosie who has ASD and doesn't understand why I can no longer walk without a stick or frame, or why I am dosed up most days. Then Reggie who is 19 months, who I can't even pick up without worrying i'll drop him or hurt because since my ops I also have been having fits!  I am not allowed to be alone with my own children because I am a risk!  

The reason I am here asking, no pleading, with people to please help me raise enough money to get to see a private Nuro Dr. Even if it's to tell me a way in which I can deal with this situation or if there is something they can do!  I don't expect miracles, I just want some kind of life back and to be a Mum to my children.  Please if you can help I would be eternally grateful and so would my family.  Thank you for taking the time to read my story Jade x
