Funeral expenses for Elise Koontz

$3,700 of $1,600 goal

Raised by 69 people in 19 months
No Longer Accepting Donations
 Share on Facebook
 Share
  Tweet
 382 shares
  • Story
  • Updates   1
Kevin-Vanessa Gore  MASSILLON, OH
The family wants to thank you all for your generosity and compassion for Elise. Any money raised above our goal will be put towards any unpaid medical expenses or any other unforeseen costs that may have been accured as a result of her death. Gods blessings to you all!

Elise Koontz was taken too soon from this world, just after celebrating her 37th birthday on November 3rd. She was a devoted daughter and pour every ouncrle of herself into her job. She will be forever remembered for her free spirit, love of music, culinary treats, and love for animals. She will be missed by all who have known her on this road of life that we are traveling. Things will never be the same without her wit, sense of style, sense of humor, and ways of caring. She will be forever missed by all of us. She was everything... Daughter, sister, aunt, boss, coworker, but most of all a friend.
With her sudden death,we are asking for any support you can give to help cover her cremation/memorial costs. We understand that this time of year is hard for everyone with the holidays but on behalf of Elise, any assistance financially or service you can provide to honor her would be greatly appreciated. We appreciate your thoughtfulness, support and prayers during this difficult time.

The Koontz FamilyFamily

Added note: the hospital is giving us just 48 hours to find a funeral home in which to send her, and no funeral home will take her with out the money up front. Please help this family. Gods blessing to you All
