      1 Week Mission Trip to Moldova

      €160 of €550 goal

      Raised by 3 people in 1 month
      Donate Now  Share on Facebook

      Created May 29, 2018
      Natalia Paredes Acuña
        Faith   München, Bayern, Deutschland
      This August I want to go to a Mission Trip to Moldova. It will be a week/long camp for children from local families and orphanages. 

      This camp will allow the children to have:
       ---> One week of a safe and encouraging environment.
       ---> The opportunity to hear of God’s love for them and what God has    done.
       ---> Multiple opportunities to give their life to Christ.
       ---> Deepen their relationship with their creator and provider.
       ---> One week to “escape” from daily life and to be children without their regular concerns such as food, care, work, lack of love, etc.

      These are some pictures of the camp in former years: 





      About me:

      I am 21 years old, I come from Colombia and I am currently a student in Germany with a student budget. Therefore I want to raise funds for my mission trip before the end of July and I know that the Lord Jesus will allow me to serve Him and these children there!

      It is going to be a great opportunity for me to show God’s love through teaching English, giving children much needed attention, having fun and sharing my personal testimony. This will also challenge me and hopefully it will help me to increase my understanding of people and their needs! 

      I would be really thankful if you can help me to raise the funds that I need, even one euro counts! Prayer is also much needed for me and my team in order to have a heart ready and prepared to serve God!
